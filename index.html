Cocoa Brownies

Recipe for the Best Fudgy Cocoa Brownies

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup unsalted butter, melted and HOT
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1.125 cup superfine sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 0.5 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 125 mL unsalted butter, melted and HOT
  • 15 mL tablespoon cooking oil
  • 280 mL superfine sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 9 mL pure vanilla extract
  • 119 mL all-purpose flour
  • 119 mL unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 mL salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C).
  2. Lightly grease an 8-inch square baking pan with cooking oil spray.
  3. Combine hot melted butter, oil and sugar together in a medium-sized bowl.
  4. Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan, smoothing the top out evenly.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the center of the brownies no longer jiggles.
  7. Remove and allow to cool to room temperature before slicing.
